Questions

What is 27aDay?
27aDay is a free golf app that surfaces the 27 best golf product recommendations on the internet every day. Products are scored by AI on discount depth, brand, demand, price, and market buzz, then explained in plain English by Chip, your AI caddie.
How does the scoring work?
Every product gets a Chip Score from 0 to 100. The score factors in discount depth, brand reputation, category demand, price point, and market buzz. Anything above 76 is elite. If Chip wouldn't recommend it to a friend, it doesn't make the 27.
How are the 27 chosen each day?
Fresh picks drop three times daily. Front 9 at 6am, Back 9 at noon, Bonus 9 at 6pm. Each session is a completely new set of products, scored and curated by Chip overnight and throughout the day.
Is 27aDay free?
Yes, 27aDay is completely free. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no premium tiers. We make money through small affiliate commissions from retailers when you buy through the app. You never pay a cent more than the listed price.
What brands does 27aDay track?
Every major OEM including Titleist, Callaway, TaylorMade, Cobra, Cleveland, Ping, and Mizuno, plus your favorite DTC and specialty brands. Over 4,000 products tracked across the golf market.
What categories does 27aDay cover?
Nine golf categories: clubs, balls, apparel, shoes, bags, rangefinders, training aids, accessories, and tech.
Who is Chip?
Chip is your AI caddie inside 27aDay. He scores every product, writes plain English explanations of why a product is worth your attention, learns what's in your bag, and recommends gear that fits your game. No marketing fluff, just real talk about real prices.
Can I set alerts for specific products or brands?
Yes. Build your Golf Locker, tell Chip what's in your bag and what you're watching, and you'll get push notifications when prices drop or new products hit that match your preferences. If nothing qualifies, Chip stays quiet.
Is my data shared or sold?
No. Your data stays in 27aDay. We don't sell it, share it, or use it for advertising. The only thing we track is what helps Chip give you better recommendations.
Is 27aDay available on Android?
27aDay is live on the Apple App Store now. Android on Google Play is coming soon.
