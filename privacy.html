Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use 27aDay, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address and password when you register
  • Golf preferences: Handicap level, preferred brands, clothing size, budget range, and other preferences you provide during onboarding
  • Usage data: How you interact with deals, which categories you browse, and app usage patterns
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize the deals and products we show you
  • To send daily deal notifications based on your preferences
  • To improve our product recommendations and deal curation
  • To maintain and improve the app
  • To communicate with you about your account

3. Affiliate Links

27aDay is an affiliate marketing platform. When you click on a deal and make a purchase, we may earn a commission from the retailer. This does not affect the price you pay. We partner with retailers including Amazon, GlobalGolf, Golfballs.com, and others.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider
  • Affiliate networks: Only anonymized click and conversion data
  • Push notification services: Device tokens for delivering notifications

5. Chip AI Conversations

When you use Chip AI, your conversations are private to you and never shared with other users. Specifically:

  • Your Chip AI conversation history is stored securely and accessible only to your account
  • No other user can see your conversations, questions, or gear preferences shared with Chip
  • Conversation data is used solely to improve your personal experience
  • We do not use your individual conversation content to train AI models or share it with third parties

6. Your Onboarding Profile

The profile you build during onboarding is personal to you:

  • Your profile is stored with row-level security, accessible only to your authenticated session
  • No other user or third party can access your profile data
  • Your profile is used exclusively to personalize your deal feed
  • You can update or delete your profile at any time from Account settings

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the Account tab. Upon deletion, your personal data will be removed within 30 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure password hashing to protect your information.

9. Children's Privacy

27aDay is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. TikTok Integration

27aDay uses TikTok's official APIs to share golf deal content on TikTok. When you authorize the TikTok integration, we access the following data under these scopes:

  • user.info.basic: Your TikTok display name and avatar
  • video.upload: Permission to upload golf deal video content
  • video.publish: Permission to publish uploaded videos

We do not access your TikTok followers, direct messages, or any data beyond the scopes listed above.
